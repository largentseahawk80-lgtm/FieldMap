# GPS Area Calculator

Quick phone GPS area calculator for field use.

## Use

1. Open the app on your phone.
2. Walk the outside perimeter in order.
3. Tap **GPS Point** at each corner or boundary point.
4. Capture 4 to 8 points.
5. Read the square footage and acres.

## Field truth

This is for fast field estimating. It is not a survey-grade legal boundary tool.

For best results, stand still at each point until GPS settles before tapping **GPS Point**.
